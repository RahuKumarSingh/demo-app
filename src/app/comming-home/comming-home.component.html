WE'RE COMMING HOME!

A very happy 70th Independence day to everyone! And this year, we're migrating home! We're coming back to where it matters.

Over the years, we have been committed to providing our customers robust services backed with the best of technology and IT infrastructure to ensure they always have their sites and applications up and running with good speeds and uptime. To this extent, till date, we have had to choose data centers in the US to host our infrastructure on. However, developments over the last few years, namely the opening of the AWS data center in Mumbai has now made it possible for us to provide the same level of reliability that we had committed to our customers in India but at a much faster access speed.

We are excited to announce that over the next couple of weeks, we will start migrating all our customers to the AWS cloud Infrastructure in the Mumbai region. We have been waiting with bated breath for several years now to see this day in action

The difference our customers will see after migration

1. Super Fast load times (low latency)

2. Increased reliability and redundancy

3. Add-on option for Global CDN

4. Lots of other new features in the highly scalable cloud environment.
